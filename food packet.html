Campaign Information

Basic Food Packet

The Basic Food Packet contains essential food items for daily consumption, including rice, cooking oil, sugar, salt, and flour. These staple ingredients are carefully selected because they are commonly used in preparing everyday meals and provide the foundation for a balanced diet. The packet is designed to support individuals or small families by helping them meet their basic nutritional needs while reducing the financial burden of purchasing essential groceries. This food packet is especially beneficial for individuals who are single, unemployed, students, senior citizens, or those experiencing temporary financial difficulties. By providing these essential items, the initiative ensures that recipients have access to the basic necessities required to prepare simple, nutritious meals at home. The Basic Food Packet reflects the campaign's commitment to supporting vulnerable communities, promoting food security, and improving the well-being of those in need.

Family Food Packet

TheFamily Food Packet contains a selection of essential food items, including rice, Milo, biscuits, cooking oil, and noodles. These items are chosen to meet the daily nutritional and dietary needs of families by providing both staple ingredients and convenient food options. Rice and cooking oil serve as the foundation for preparing daily meals, while noodles offer a quick and practical meal option. Milo and biscuits provide additional energy and nutrition, making them suitable for both adults and children. This food packet is specially designed for households with multiple family members, particularly those facing financial challenges or requiring temporary assistance. By supplying these essential groceries, the Family Food Packet helps reduce the cost of daily living and ensures that families have access to sufficient food during difficult times. Through this initiative, the campaign aims to promote food security, support family well-being, and provide meaningful assistance to communities in need.

Premium Food Packet

The Premium Food Packet contains a premium selection of food items,rice, coffee ,sugar, flour and milk. These items are carefully chosen to provide a more indulgent and varied dining experience while still meeting the nutritional needs of families. The packet is designed for households that appreciate quality ingredients and want to enhance their daily meals with superior products. This food packet is ideal for families who wish to treat themselves occasionally or for those who have a higher income and can afford premium food options. By supplying these high-quality groceries, the Premium Food Packet helps ensure that families have access to delicious and nutritious meals that cater to their taste preferences and dietary requirements. Through this initiative, the campaign aims to promote culinary excellence, support family satisfaction, and provide an enhanced dining experience for communities in need.
